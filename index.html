County Validation Notes
v3.0.0 - default Full 254 TX county enum (county-only, no subdivisions)

About CATCH
Contract & Schema Triage Hub for courts data validation, reporting, and recurring issue review
Version 3.1
What it does
One validator, two audiences
CATCH helps technical users diagnose a single failed submission and helps PMs monitor saved envelopes, recurring issues, and publisher pressure across the portfolio.
Single
Payload review
Best for validating one envelope and confirming exactly what failed.
Portfolio
PM queue
Best for envelope-level review, prioritization, and ticket creation.
History
Technical archive
Best for reopening saved runs, exporting, backup, and restore.
Trends
Pattern reporting
Best for recurring issue analysis across publishers, fields, and entity types.
How Teams Use It
Project managersUse Portfolio to identify which envelopes need tickets, which publishers are driving issues, and what was sent incorrectly.
Support and TPM teamsUse Single, History, and schema review to diagnose specific submissions and confirm contract expectations.
LeadershipUse Trends and the Health Report to monitor recurring issue pressure and publisher concentration over time.
What CATCH Flags
Missing fieldsRequired fields that were not included in the payload.
Type mismatchesValues sent in the wrong format, such as text where a number is expected.
Invalid valuesValues that do not match approved contract enums or publisher scope.
Extra or wrong fieldsFields that do not belong on the entity or appear under the wrong field name.
Storage and Export
Browser storageSaved history, trend data, and refinements live locally in the browser unless exported or cleared.
Health reportingUse Health Report for portfolio-level workbook output and JSON for raw archive handoff.
Backup and restoreUse Backup and Import to preserve history across machines or browser resets.
